Missions

Assistance and care.

Les Petits Frères are assisting person, in priority over 50 years old, suffering from isolation, poverty, sickness, exclusion.

To assist is to welcome persons who were sidelined because of age, state of health or lifestyle, to create genuine relationships, to revive joy and to face with them the daily sufferings, the dread of solitude.
To throw a birthday party, share a meal and bring them all the help needed, the support and protection necessary and a decent home.
To be there even when there is not solution, to be close until the end, to care in the last moment of life.
To live with them a holiday, a dream coming true …

That is what make sense to all these actions aiming at these persons. Welcome, visit, organise collective animations (Christmas eves, holidays, birthdays…), protect, host, accommodate… All theses actions only give sense when linked to this relation of free, uninterrupted friendship.

To assist is to recognize the person and to accept his uniqueness, to respect his dignity, his intimacy, his mystery. It’s to be be his witness, his friend. It’s to value what he’s living and to help him discover his hidden potential, to give him the opportunity to express his desires and deep aspirations. It’s to walk side by side while respecting his evolution and own rhythm, to adjust constantly to his needs. It’s to help him to take charge, to live him to his own choices.

It’s to consider him like someone still in the making.
